1. english essay negative review about restaurant


Jawaban:

Recently, I visited a restaurant in the heart of the city and unfortunately, I had an abysmal experience that compelled me to write this negative review. The establishment, despite its fancy decor, failed to deliver a satisfactory dining experience to its customers.

Firstly, the service was ghastly slow. It took a considerably long time for the staff to take our order and for the food to arrive at our table. Furthermore, the staff appeared to be disinterested and least bothered about their customers. Their attitudes were lackluster and their service was poor.

Secondly, the food was a massive letdown. The presentation was unsatisfactory and the flavors were underwhelming. The dishes I ordered lacked proper seasoning, and the ingredients used were subpar. Even the dessert that we ordered failed to impress us and tasted stale.

Lastly, the prices did not justify the quality or quantity of the food. The portions were small and overpriced, and the quality was poor. I felt cheated at the end of my meal, as the prices were exorbitant for the quality we had received.

In conclusion, the entire dining experience was lackluster and highly disappointing. From the slow and uninterested service to the overpriced and underwhelming food, it is safe to say that I would not recommend this restaurant to anyone. There are several other restaurants in the city that offer much better food, service, and value for money.

16. contoh soal essay elektronika​


Tentu, berikut beberapa contoh soal essay dalam bidang elektronika:

Jelaskan prinsip kerja dari dioda p-n junction. Bagaimana dioda berperan dalam sirkuit elektronik?

Apakah yang dimaksud dengan transistor? Jelaskan jenis-jenis transistor dan aplikasi praktis dari masing-masing jenis.

Jelaskan perbedaan antara resistor dan kapasitor dalam sirkuit elektronik. Berikan contoh kasus di mana keduanya digunakan dalam rangkaian yang berbeda.

Apa yang dimaksud dengan hambatan (resistansi) dalam sirkuit listrik? Bagaimana cara menghitung hambatan total dalam rangkaian seri dan paralel?

Jelaskan prinsip kerja dari transformator dan bagaimana transformator digunakan dalam pengaturan tegangan dalam pembangkit listrik.

Apa yang dimaksud dengan kode warna pada resistor? Bagaimana Anda dapat mengidentifikasi nilai hambatan sebuah resistor berdasarkan kode warna tersebut?

Bagaimana prinsip kerja dari sensor suhu termokopel? Jelaskan penggunaannya dalam aplikasi yang memerlukan pengukuran suhu yang akurat.

Apa yang dimaksud dengan sistem digital? Jelaskan perbedaan antara sistem digital dan sistem analog. Berikan contoh penggunaan masing-masing sistem dalam kehidupan sehari-hari.

Bagaimana cara menghitung daya dalam sirkuit listrik? Jelaskan hubungan antara tegangan, arus, dan daya dalam sebuah resistor.

Jelaskan prinsip dasar dari rangkaian flip-flop dalam elektronika digital. Bagaimana flip-flop digunakan dalam pembentukan sirkuit-sirkuit logika seperti register geser dan penghitung?

Pastikan untuk menjawab setiap pertanyaan dengan jelas dan memberikan contoh atau ilustrasi jika diperlukan untuk mendukung jawaban Anda.

Simple Past Tense

Simple past tense digunakan untuk menyatakan suatu kejadian di waktu tertentu di masa lampau.

Time expression: yesterday, last two days, last year, this morning, a week ago, ...etc.

Formula:

Verbal sentences:

(+)  Subject + Verb2

(-)   Subject + did + not + Verb1  

(?)  Did + Subject + Verb1?

Nominal sentence:

(+)  Subject + to be (was/were) + O/C

(-)   Subject + to be (was/ were) + O/C  

(?)  To be (was/ were) +Subject + O/C?

Contoh :

1. (+) Susi took a bath yesterday

  (-) Susi did not take a bath yesterday

  (?) Did Susi take a bath yesterday ?

2. (+) Susi was an artist

    (-) Susi was not an artist

    (?) Was Susi an artist ?

3. (+) Susi brought her dictionary  

    (-) Susi did not bring her dictionary  

    (?) Did Susi bring her dictionary ?

 4. (+) My father drank a cup of coffee this morning

    (-) My father did not drink a cup of coffee this morning

    (?) Did My father drink a cup of coffee this morning?

5. (+) Amy swam in the swimming pool yesterday

   (-) Amy did not swim in the swimming pool yesterday

   (?) Did Amy swim in the swimming pool yesterday?

6. (+) you went to bed at 9 last night

    (-) you did not go to bed at 9 last night

    (?)Did you go to bed at 9 last night?

7. (+) She bought dresses in that supermarket two days ago

   (-) She did not buy dresses in that supermarket two days ago

   (?) Did She buy dresses in that supermarket two days ago?

8. (+) My teacher wrote with a pen, but we wrote with pencils  

   (-) My teacher did not write with a pen, but we did not write with pencils  

   (?) Did My teacher write with a pen, but did we write with pencils?

9.(+) My mom was very diligent

Report text tentang Aurora Phenomena

Aurora is a natural phenomena that refers to northern light, southern light and polar light. It is a colorful natural light display that appears in the sky at the evening and the night, seen in the sky of Arctic and Antartic region.

Because the aurora is only found at those regions, people from different area have to spend much money and should prepare their physics to be able to see it directly.

The aurora has complex mix color that will change everyday. The beauty is surely a thousand times better than fireworks in the new year time.

That is why some people dreamed to see it.

Unfortunately, the way to see it at the arctic and antartic regions is too much risky and dangerous and perhaps only a few people can do this including some experts and researchers.

The detail explanation on this phenomena is not fully understood, but the cause of aurora is basically involves the interaction between the solar wind with the earth’s magnetosphere.

First, the storms on the sun send gust of charged solar particle. When the earth is in the path of that particle stream, then the erath’s magnetosphere reacts.

At that time, the earth electrons move to higher-energy orbits or further away from the nucleus. After that, the electrons move back to a lower-energy orbit.

This process will release the particle light or photon which creates aurora.

Whether or not this religious inspiration is true, it is known that the piece is not the only Starry Nightpainting that Van Gogh ever created. Gogh was quite proud of a piece he had painted earlier in Arles in 1888 that depicted stars reflecting in the Rhone River. Like Starry Night this previous piece shares many of the qualities that have made Starry Nightsuch a popular painting. For instance:

The stars in the night sky are surrounded with their own orb of light.The Reflection of artificial light (new to the time period) from Arles in the river makes the one's eyes move around the painting; thus keeping the viewer visually involved.There are structures in the distant lit up in a warm glow of light.

Starry Night over the Rhone contains one final aspect that is not featured in the Starry Night piece; humans. In the bottom right corner of the painting there is a couple walking along the river. This gives the painting a down to earth feel with a natural quality. Although this painting is not nearly as popular as the seconded Starry Night piece, it still creates a pleasurable and life like environment to look at. It is also resides as a very sought after piece when it is placed with Starry Night and the following painting to create a montage of Van Gogh's Starry Night works.

Jawaban:

1. Jelaskan apa yang dimaksud dengan komponen aktif dan komponen pasif dalam elektronika?

Komponen aktif adalah komponen elektronika yang dapat menghasilkan arus listrik atau tegangan listrik. Komponen pasif adalah komponen elektronika yang hanya dapat mengalirkan arus listrik atau tegangan listrik.

Beberapa contoh komponen aktif adalah transistor, dioda, dan op-amp. Beberapa contoh komponen pasif adalah resistor, kapasitor, dan induktor.

2. Sebutkan 3 jenis dioda dan jelaskan fungsinya masing-masing!

Ada tiga jenis dioda, yaitu:

* Dioda pemancar cahaya (LED) berfungsi untuk menghasilkan cahaya.

* Dioda penyearah berfungsi untuk menyearahkan arus listrik bolak-balik menjadi arus listrik searah.

* Dioda Zener berfungsi untuk membatasi tegangan listrik.

3. Apa yang dimaksud dengan transistor? Jelaskan cara kerja transistor sebagai penguat dan sebagai saklar!

Transistor adalah komponen elektronika yang terdiri dari tiga buah lapisan semikonduktor. Transistor dapat bekerja sebagai penguat dan sebagai saklar.

Transistor sebagai penguat bekerja dengan cara memperkuat arus listrik atau tegangan listrik yang masuk ke dalamnya. Transistor sebagai saklar bekerja dengan cara mematikan atau menghidupkan arus listrik.

4. Gambarkan dan jelaskan rangkaian RC!

Rangkaian RC adalah rangkaian yang terdiri dari resistor dan kapasitor. Rangkaian RC dapat digunakan untuk menyaring sinyal listrik, menunda waktu, dan mengubah bentuk sinyal listrik.

5. Sebutkan 3 jenis kapasitor dan jelaskan perbedaannya!

Ada tiga jenis kapasitor, yaitu:

* Kapasitor elektrolit berfungsi untuk menyimpan muatan listrik dalam jumlah besar.

* Kapasitor keramik berfungsi untuk menyimpan muatan listrik dalam jumlah kecil.

* Kapasitor film berfungsi untuk menyimpan muatan listrik dalam jumlah sedang.

6. Apa yang dimaksud dengan induktor? Jelaskan cara kerja induktor!

Induktor adalah komponen elektronika yang dapat menyimpan energi listrik dalam bentuk medan magnet. Induktor dapat digunakan untuk menyaring sinyal listrik, membangkitkan osilasi listrik, dan mengubah bentuk sinyal listrik.

7. Gambarkan dan jelaskan rangkaian LC!

Rangkaian LC adalah rangkaian yang terdiri dari induktor dan kapasitor. Rangkaian LC dapat digunakan untuk menghasilkan osilasi listrik.

8. Sebutkan 3 jenis resistor dan jelaskan perbedaannya!

Ada tiga jenis resistor, yaitu:

* Resistor tetap berfungsi untuk menahan arus listrik dalam jumlah tetap.

* Resistor variabel berfungsi untuk mengatur besar hambatan resistor.

* Resistor peka suhu berfungsi untuk mengubah besar hambatan resistor sesuai dengan suhu.

9. Apa yang dimaksud dengan sumber tegangan? Jelaskan jenis-jenis sumber tegangan!

Sumber tegangan adalah komponen elektronika yang menghasilkan tegangan listrik. Sumber tegangan dapat berupa baterai, adaptor, atau generator.

Ada dua jenis sumber tegangan, yaitu:

* Sumber tegangan searah (DC) menghasilkan tegangan listrik yang arahnya selalu sama.

* Sumber tegangan bolak-balik (AC) menghasilkan tegangan listrik yang arahnya bolak-balik.

10. Sebutkan 3 jenis filter dan jelaskan fungsinya masing-masing!

Ada tiga jenis filter, yaitu:

* Filter pasif berfungsi untuk menyaring sinyal listrik berdasarkan frekuensinya.

* Filter aktif berfungsi untuk menyaring sinyal listrik berdasarkan frekuensinya dan memperkuat sinyal listrik.

* Filter digital berfungsi untuk menyaring sinyal listrik berdasarkan frekuensinya menggunakan metode digital.

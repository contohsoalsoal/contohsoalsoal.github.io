20. contoh kalimat simple past,past continuous,past perfect,past p.continus,future, future continuous, future perfect,f.p cont,past future,p.f.continouse,past perfect continuous,​


Here are examples of each tense:

Simple Past:

I watched a movie last night.She cooked dinner for us yesterday.They visited their grandparents on the weekend.

Past Continuous:

I was studying when the phone rang.He was playing football while it was raining.We were talking about our plans for the weekend.

Past Perfect:

She had already finished her homework before dinner.They had visited the museum before it closed.He had gone to the store before it started raining.

Past Perfect Continuous:

She had been studying for three hours before taking a break.They had been waiting for the bus for half an hour before it arrived.He had been working on the project all day before submitting it.

Future:

I will go to the beach next weekend.She will visit her family during the holidays.They will attend the concert next month.

Future Continuous:

I will be studying for my exams tomorrow.He will be working on his presentation all morning.They will be traveling to Europe next year.

Future Perfect:

She will have finished her project by the end of the week.They will have graduated from college in a few months.He will have completed the course before starting the new job.

Future Perfect Continuous:

She will have been studying English for two years by next summer.They will have been living in the city for five years by next month.He will have been working at the company for ten years by his retirement.

Past Future:

She said she would help me with my project tomorrow.They promised they would call me later in the evening.He told me he would come to the party next week.

Past Future Continuous:

She said she would be studying all night for the exam.They promised they would be working on the project until it's finished.He told me he would be traveling for the next two weeks.

I hope these examples help clarify each tense for you!

22. past continuous tense (+),(-),(?)preset continuous tense (+),(-),(?)future continuous tense (+),(-),(?)contoh:​


Jawaban:

Past continuous tense

(+) I was watching a new movie on Netflix when my mother came home last night.

(-) I was not watching a new movie on Netflix when my mother came home last night.

(?) Was I watching a new movie on Netflix when my mother came home  last night?

Preset continuous tense

(+) Our school principal is giving his speech at the moment.

(-) Our school principal is not giving his speech at the moment.

(?) Is our school principal giving his speech at the moment?

Future continuous tense

(+) I will be preparing the report by the time you arrive tomorrow morning.

(-) I will not be preparing the report by the time you arrive tomorrow morning.

(?) Will I be preparing the report by the time you arrive tomorrow morning?

Penjelasan:

Ketiga tenses ini mengandung kata "continuous" (berkelanjutan/terus-menerus) yang mengandung makna bahwa yang dikerjakan itu masih berlangsung.

Dalam Past continuous tense, kondisi masih berlangsung itu dilihat pada suatu titik di masa lampau. (Dalam contoh si atas adalah "when my mother came home last night" (ketika ibuku pulang tadi malam). Jadi kalimat ini bermakna bahwa ketika ibu pulang tadi malam, saya sedang menonton film . Jadi kegiatan menonton itu masih berlangsung ketika ibu pulang, tadi malam (masa lampau)

Dalam Present continuous tense, kondisi masih berlangsung itu dilihat pada suatu titik masa kini atau sekarang (yaitu pada titik waktu ketika sipenutur mengucapkan kata ini).

Contoh kalimat di atas: Our school principal is giving his speech at the moment. (kepala sekolah saat ini sedang berpidato).

Skenarionya misalnya kamu sedang ada di sekolah mendengarkan pidato kepala sekolah, lalu kamu ditelpon mama, lalu kamu bilang "Mom, our school principal is giving his speech at the moment"

Dalam Future continuous tense, kita membayangkan/memperkirakan bahwa di satu  titik waktu di masa depan sesuatu sedang terjadi/dikerjakan.

Dalam contoh di atas: I will be preparing the report by the time you arrive tomorrow moring.

Artinya besok pagi pas waktu kamu tiba, saya akan sedang melakukan sesuatu yaitu menyiapkan laporan.

Untuk rumus ada di buku pelajaran.

26. soal past continuous mau nguji aja ​


PERTANYAAN & SOAL :

1. Were you ... this picture at 8 o'clock yesterday? (paint)

= Were you painting this picture at 8 o'clock yesterday?

2. This morning, Yesterday, The rain ... Harder and harder. (get)

= This morning, Yesterday, The rain was getting Harder and harder.

3. My mother ... This fruit to Farikha at eight o'clock last night. (give)

= My mother was giving This fruit to Farikha at eight o'clock last night.

4. We... the chicken in front of the bridge at seven o'clock last night. (catch)

= We were catching the chicken in front of the bridge at seven o'clock last night.

5. Was Salisa... my house at this time yesterday? (visit)

= Was Salisa visiting my house at this time yesterday?

6. I ... a new car at this time last month. (buy)

= I was buying a new car at this time last month.

7. Tamara and Silvia... to Malang at this time last month. (go)

= Tamara and Silvia were going to Malang at this time last month.

8. Sabrina ... English at 8 o'clock last night. (learn)

= Sabrina was learning English at 8 o'clock last night.

9. Yesterday morning, They ... me in this market. (help)

= Yesterday morning, They were helping me in this market.

10. My mother ... a bowl of rice when I bought a banana. (cook)

= My mother was cooking a bowl of rice when I bought a banana.

11. Tamara ... a letter to his boyfriend at 7 o'clock yesterday. (write)

= Tamara was writing a letter to his boyfriend at 7 o'clock yesterday.

12. Was usman... in this store at 10 o'clock last night? (eat)

= Was usman eating in this store at 10 o'clock last night?

13. Were they.. this recipe at this time yesterday? (make)

= Were they making this recipe at this time yesterday?

14. I invited Sabrina at this event last, but Sabrina ... to us. (come)

= I invited Sabrina at this event last, but Sabrina was not coming to us.

PENJELASAN :

Past continuous terdiri dari dua kata yaitu past dan continuous. "Past" artinya masa lampau. Penjelasan

1. They are working too hard today, do you see it? - Di sini kalimat menunjukkan bahwa mereka (mereka yang malas) sebenarnya sedang bekerja keras. Frase "are working" mengindikasikan bahwa tindakan bekerja sedang berlangsung pada saat ini.

2. I am giving her a flower now. - Kalimat ini menunjukkan bahwa pembicara (I) sedang memberikan bunga kepada seseorang (her) pada saat ini. "Am giving" menunjukkan bahwa tindakan memberi sedang berlangsung pada saat ini.

3. Anita is doing the examination today, pray for her success. - Frase "is doing" menunjukkan bahwa saat ini Anita sedang melakukan ujian. Kalimat ini juga meminta untuk mendoakan keberhasilan Anita dalam ujian tersebut.

4. You are making a lot of noise, could you be quieter? - Kalimat ini mengatakan bahwa "you" sedang membuat banyak kebisingan pada saat ini. Permintaan "could you be quieter?" menunjukkan bahwa pembicara meminta agar "you" menjadi lebih tenang.

5. What are you doing in this place? - Pertanyaan ini menanyakan apa yang "you" (yang sedang di dalam tempat ini) sedang lakukan saat ini. "Are you doing" menunjukkan bahwa pembicara ingin mengetahui tindakan yang sedang berlangsung.

6. Is Sabrina working today? - Pertanyaan ini menanyakan apakah Sabrina sedang bekerja hari ini. "Is working" menunjukkan bahwa tindakan bekerja untuk Sabrina sedang berlangsung pada saat ini.

7. Why are you looking at me like that? - Kalimat ini menanyakan mengapa "you" sedang menatap pembicara dengan cara tertentu. "Are looking" menunjukkan bahwa tindakan menatap sedang berlangsung pada saat ini.

8. What are you eating in this kitchen? - Pertanyaan ini menanyakan apa yang "you" sedang makan di dapur ini. "Are you eating" menunjukkan bahwa pembicara ingin mengetahui tindakan makan yang sedang berlangsung.

9. When am I going today? - Pertanyaan ini menanyakan kapan pembicara akan pergi pada hari ini. "Am going" menunjukkan bahwa tindakan pergi akan dilakukan oleh pembicara pada saat ini.

10. Toldo and Rudi are playing football in the yard. - Kalimat ini mengatakan bahwa Toldo dan Rudi sedang bermain sepak bola di halaman. "Are playing" menunjukkan bahwa tindakan bermain sedang berlangsung pada saat ini.

11. Amelia is singing Dandung song in her house. - Kalimat ini mengatakan bahwa Amelia sedang menyanyikan lagu Dandung di rumahnya. "Is singing" menunjukkan bahwa tindakan menyanyi sedang berlangsung pada saat ini.

12. Is he knocking her wall? - Pertanyaan ini menanyakan apakah dia sedang mengetuk temboknya. "Is knocking" menunjukkan bahwa tindakan mengetuk sedang berlangsung pada saat ini.

13. Where are they working this month? - Pertanyaan ini menanyakan di mana mereka bekerja bulan ini. "Are they working" menunjukkan bahwa tindakan bekerja sedang berlangsung pada saat ini.

14. Your cat is eating the mouse in this house. - Kalimat ini mengatakan bahwa kucingmu sedang makan tikus di rumah ini. "Is eating" menunjukkan bahwa tindakan makan sedang berlangsung pada saat ini.

15. "How is your study?" "Not so good at the moment. I am enjoying it very much." - Jawaban ini merespon pertanyaan "Bagaimana belajarmu?". "Am enjoying" menunjukkan bahwa saat ini pembicara sedang menikmati belajar.

38. contoh kalimat simple past menjadi past continuous dan past perfect

I was studying English yesterday (Past Continues)
I had studied English (Past Perfect)

2. Tony met his childhood friend after he finished his Midterm test. (simple past tense)
Tony was meet his childhood friend after he finishing his Midterm test. (Past Continues)
Tony met his childhood friend after he had finished his Midterm test.

3. He didn't come to Anita's party last night.
He wasn't coming to Anita's party last night.
He hadn't came to Anita's party last night

SIMPLE PAST Or PAST CONTINUOUS

1. When my father went home, my mother was preparing our dinner.

2. The girl was playing with her dolls when I passed in front of her house.

3. While you were disccusing the lesson, she was at office.

4. When the woman called me, I was talking to her husband.

EXPLANATION

Kita sering menggunakan "Past Continuous" dan "Simple Past" dalam satu kalimat yang sama seperti soal yang ditanyakan di atas. Baik "Simple past" maupun "Past Continuous" menjabarkan kejadian pada masa lampau.

Rumus:

Simple past ➡ S+verb2+ O/C

Past continuous ➡ S+to be(was/were)+Verb-ing+O/C

-

Pada umumnya, kejadian pada "Simple past" itu pendek dan tidak terduga. Kejadian ini menginterupsi kejadian lama yang biasanya menggunakan "Past Continuous."

Contoh:

I was washing the clothes when my friend phoned me.

Induk klausa menggunakan pola "Past Continuous" (Washing the clothes). Ini menandakan kejadiannya berlangsung lebih lama daripada yang menggunakan pola "Simple past" (phoned me). Aksi yang menggunakan "Simple past" pendek dan menginterupsi aksi yang menggunakan "Past continuous."

Ketika dalam satu kalimat terdapat aksi "Simple past" dan "Past continuous" biasanya menggunakan penghubung "When" atau "While."

Penghubung "When" diikuti oleh kalimat bentuk "Simple past." Penghubung "While" diikuti oleh kalimat bentuk "Past Continuous." Selain itu, bisa digunakan untuk dua aksi yang berlangsung bersamaan.

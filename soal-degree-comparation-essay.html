1. apa yang dimaksud dengan degree comparation?


Degree comparation adalah bentuk adjective atau adverb yang menyatakan perbandingan.degree comparation adl membandingkan dua org atau dua benda yg berbeda dg menggunakan kata sifat.

2. rumus dari degree of comparation


DEGREE OF COMPARISON


Degree of Comparison consists of  :

Positive DegreeComperative DegreeSuperlative Degree 


1. POSITIVE DEGREE

Positive Degree shows common characteristic of objects. For instance : as tall as, as expensive as, as old as and the others.
Pattern :


Subject + to be + as + adjective + as .....


Example :
1. This dictionary is as expensive as mine.
2. His brother is as clever as my sister.
3. This book is as thick as that one.

2. COMPERATIVE DEGREE

Comperative Degree is used to show different characteristic of objects. For instance : bigger, taller, older and the others. Comperative Degree is formed by adding -er behind the adjective, if the adjective consists of 1 syllable.

Pattern :


Subject + to be + adjective + er + than .....


Example :

1. This dictionary is cheaper than mine.
2. Your father is older than John's father.
3. Linda is prettier than her elder sister.


     If the adjective consists of 2 syllable or more, Comperative Degree isn't formed by adding -er behind the adjective, but Comperative Degree is formed by adding "more" in front of the adjective.

Pattern :


Subject + to be + more + adjective + than .....


Example :
1. Your book is more expensive than mine.
2. This lesson is more difficult than that one.
3. English is more important than Spanish.

3. SUPERLATIVE DEGREE

Superlative Degree is used to show levels "most" of objects. For instance : most diligent, the youngest, the oldest and the others. Superlative Degree is formed by adding -est behind the adjective if the adjective consists of 1 syllable.

Rumus  :


Subject + to be + the + adjective + est .....


Example : 

  1. He is the cleverest among them all.
  2. Jones is the oldest student in our class.
  3. You are the laziest member in our club.


     If the adjective consists of 2 syllable or more, Superlative Degree isn't formed by adding -est to the adjective, but Superlative Degree is formed by adding "most" in front of adjective.

Pattern :


Subject + to be + the + most + adjective .....


Example :

1. Jones is the most intelligent student
2. This is the most beautiful flower.
3. That is the most expensive shirt.

16. 4. complate the degrees of comparation table​


comparative adjective adalah kata sifat yang digunakan untuk membandingkan 2 objek dengan menambahkan -er, atau more

penggunaan -er :

-er digunakan jika kata sifatnya hanya memiliki 1 atau 2 suku kata. contohnya :

- short ---> shorter

- deep ---> deeper

- pretty ---> prettier

- good ---> better

penggunaan more :

more digunakan jika kata sifatnya memiliki 3 suku kata atau lebih. contohnya :

- important ---> more important

- dangerous ---> more dangerous

- serious ---> more serious

superlative adjective adalah kata sifat yang digunakan untuk mengungkapkan perbandingan tingkat yang paling tinggi dengan menambahkan -est, atau most.

penggunaan -est :

sama seperti yang comparative, penggunaan -est digunakan jika kata sifatnya hanya memiliki 1 atau 2 suku kata. contohnya :

- lonely ---> loneliest

- big ---> biggest

penggunaan most :

digunakan jika kata sifatnya memiliki 3 suku kata atau lebih. contohnya :

- important ---> most important

- dangerous ---> most dangerous

Degrees of comparison diterapkan hanya untuk kata sifat. Ada 3 jenis dari degrees of compression yaitu : Positive degree ( tingkatan biasa ) , Comparative Degree ( Tingkatan Lebih ) , Superlative Degree (Tingkatan Paling).

3 Bentuk perbandingan yang bisa kita gunakan dalam kalimat adalah :

A)Comparative Degree

ialah bentuk perbandingan yang digunakan apabila kita ingin membandingkan dua kalimat yang berbeda dengan menggunakan kata " than "

Example :

Siti is fifteen years oldBudi Is seventeen years old

Comparative :

Siti is younger than BudiBudi is older than Siti

Ialah perbandingan yang digunakan apabila kita ingin membandingkan lebih dari 2 kalimat yang berbeda dengan menggunakan kata " of the ".

Example :

Siti is fifteen years oldBudi is seventeen years oldRudi is twenty years old

Comparative :

Siti is the youngest of the threeBudi is the oldest of the treeC)As.....as/so.....as (tingkatan sama)

Ialah bentuk perbandingan yang digunakan apabila kita ingin membandingkan dari dua kalimat yang sama di dalam sebuah kalimat.

Example :

Siti is as young as BudiBudi is as old as Siti

In Question :

Are you as all my friend ?Yes, I am as old yes my friendNo , I am not so old as my friend

There are 3 ways to make degrees of comparison =

• For the work has one or two syllables. adding "er" comparative adding "The....est" in superlative.

Example:

Positive = Strong , HandsomeComparative = Stronger , HandsomerSuperlative = The Strongest , The Handsomest

• For the word is adding "y". Change "y" + "er" in Comparative. "the" + " I " + est " in superlative.

Example:

Positive = Lazy , BuzyComparative = Lazier , BusierSuperlative = The Laziest , The Busiest

• For the word has more than two syllables. Adding "more" in Comparative. "the most" in Superlative.

Example:

Positive = Beatiful , Difficult Comparative = More Beatiful , More DifficultSuperlative = The Most Beatiful , The Most Difficult

34. DEGREE OF COMPARATION OF ADJECTIVEBuatlah comparative degree dari kalimat dibawah ini!1. Mr. Asja is as smart as Mr. John- Comparative = ............- Superlative = ........2. Mrs. Betty is more beautiful than Talita- Positive = .......- Superlative = .........pleasee tolong bantu​


The Degree of Comparison of the sentence is :

1. Mr. Asja is as smart as Mr. John

Comparative = Mr. Asja is smarter than Mr. JohnSuperlative = Mr. Asja is smartest than Mr. John

2. Mrs. Betty is more beautiful than Talita

Positive = Mrs. Betty is as beautiful as TalitaSuperlative = Mrs. Betty is most beautiful than Talita

○●○●○●○●○●○●○●○●○●○●○●○●○●○●○●○

EXPLANATION

Degree of comparison is an adjective that changes form and is used to compare one thing or some people with another.

In a Degree of Comparison, an adjective or an adverb will change its form according to the level of comparison. Therefore, the Comparison Degree can also be referred to as the Comparison Degree.

Degree of Comparison Function

Degree of Comparison is used when you want to compare one thing with another.

Degree of Comparison Level

Degree of Comparison has three levels : namely :

Positive degreeComparative degreeSuperlative degree.

Degree of Comparison Patterns

1. Positive Degree

In the positive degree, the two things being compared are at the same or comparable level, therefore they are used as adjectives/adverbs as. For example, "You are as pretty as her." means you are as beautiful as her.

2. Comparative Degree

In the comparative degree, one of the two things being compared is 'more' than the other, that's why an adjective/adverb + -er or more + adjective/adverb is used. For example, "You are prettier than her." means you are prettier than her.

3. Superlative Degree

In the superlative degree, the thing being discussed has the most 'most' trait among others, therefore adjective/adverb + -est or most + adjective/adverb are used. For example, "You are the prettiest girl in school." means you are the most beautiful girl in school.

The use of -er/-est

The use of -er/-est or more/most must be adjusted to the adjective/adverb used in the sentence.

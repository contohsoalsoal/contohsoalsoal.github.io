Jawaban:

present tense

1. [Active Voice] My little sister asks the waiter to bring a water. (Adikku meminta pelayan untuk membawakan segelas air)

[Passive Voice] The waiter is asked by my little sister to bring a water. (Pelayan dimintai adikku untuk membawakan segelas air)

2. [Active Voice] Galang eats fried rice every moring. (Gembul makan nasi goreng pagi ini)

[Passive Voice] Fried rice is eaten by galang every morning. (Nasi goreng dimakan Gembul pagi ini)

3.[Active Voice] I ride a bike with my friends (Aku mengendarai sepeda dengan teman temanku)

[Passive Voice] The bike is ridden by me with my friends. (Sepeda dikendarai olehku bersama teman teman ku)

4. [Active Voice] Bambang washes those friuts in the river. (Bambang mencuci buah-buahan di sungai)

[Passive Voice] Those fruits are washed by Bambang in the river. (Buah-buahan dicuci bambang di sungai)

5. [Active Voice] I read some books every day. (Aku membaca beberapa buku setiap hari)

[Passive Voice] Some books are red by me everyday

past simple tense

1. [Active Voice] I bought this shoes yesterday. (Aku membeli sepatu ini kemarin)

[Passive Voice] This shoes was bought by me yesterday. (Sepatu ini dibeli olehku kemarin)

2. [Active Voice] She called me last night (Dia menelponku tadi malam)

[Passive Voice] I was called last night by her (Aku ditelpon tadi malam olehnya)

3. [Active Voice] Dibyo played soccer yesterday. (Dibyo bermain sepak bola kemarin)

[Passive Voice] Soccer was played by Dibyo yesterday. (Sepak bola dimainkan oleh Dibyo kemarin)

4.[Active Voice] I met them in the park. (Aku bertemu dengan mereka di taman)

[Passive Voice] They were met by me in the park. (Mereka ditemukan olehku di taman)

5. [Active Voice] She sent me a message an hour ago (Dia mengirim pesan padaku sejam yg lalu)

[Passive Voice] I was sent a message by her an hour ago. (Aku dikirimi pesan olehnya sejam yg lalu)

22. Berikan 10 kalimat passive voice beserta structure passive voice thanks :)


formula passive voice : S (direct object) + was/were + past participle + /-by
formula diatas untuk transitive verb
passive voice : S (object of preposition) + was/were + past participle + preposition + /-by ... (agent)
formula diatas untuk intransitive verb
ex :
1. The letter was left when he was away from his desk
2. sardines and rice were eaten for dinner by Tio

rumus kalimat pasif voice:
Subject penerima/Object + to be (sesuai tenses) + Verb III + by + subject pelaku + keterangan waktu
tak kasih contoh nya ya:
1. A car is driven by Andi
2. Lia was meet by Ani yesterday
3. A car is being washed by Tarjo
4. The cake was being made in the kitchen
5. A letter is written by olive

40. bagaimana cara membuat kalimat active voice dan passive voice


Kalimat "active voice" adalah kalimat yang berbuat sesuatu atau melakukan suatu pekerjaan.
Rumus:
1. Subyek + Predikat (kata kerja/verb) + Objek
contoh: The cat bites a mouse.
2. Subyek + to be + kata sifat/adjective
contoh: You are beautiful.
3. Subyek + to be + kata benda/noun
contoh: Indonesian is my lovely country.

Kalimat "passive voice" adalah kalimat yang subyeknya dikenai suatu pekerjaan atau menderita sesuatu. Kalimai pasif harus berbentuk Past Participle.
Rumus:
Present Tense
Objek + to be (is, am, are) +  Past Participle (Verb ke-III + by + Subyek)
contoh: A mouse is bitten by the cat.

Present Continues Tense
Objek + to be + being + Past Participle
Contoh:
Aktif: Mother is cooking the red velvet cake
Pasif: The red velvet cake is being cooked by mother.

Present Perfect Tense
Objek + have/has + been + Past Participle
Contoh:
Aktif: I have closed the door.
Pasif: The door have been closed by me.

Present Perfect Continues Tense
Objek + have/has + been + being + Past Participle
Contoh:
Aktif: Natasya has been writing the animal production lesson.
Pasif: The animal production lesson has been being written by Natasya.

Simple Past Tense
.Objek + was/were + Past Participle
Contoh:
Aktif: Pandu painted the wall, yesterday.
Pasif: The wall was painted by Pandu, yesterday.

Past Continues Tense
Objek + was/were + being + Past Participle
Contoh:
Aktif: Tita was eating a piece of pizza.
Pasif: A piece of pizza was being ate by Tita.

Past Perfect Tense
Objek + had been + Past Participle
Contoh:
Aktif: She has taken her fondou set.
Pasif: The fondou set had been taken by her.

1. Essay : Lengkapi Tenses berikut :1. (+) I am going to your home tomorrow(-) ......(?) ........?​


(+) I am going to your home tomorrow

(-) I am not going to your home tomorrow

(?) Am I going to your home tomorrow?

Penjelasan

(+) S + be + going to + ...

(-) S + be + not + going to + ...

(?) Be + S + going to + ... + ?

Note

S (Subject) = I, you, etcBe = am/is/are

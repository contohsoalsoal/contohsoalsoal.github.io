3. contoh soal essay tentang sugestion dan offer dan penjelasannya​


Contoh soal essay tentang sugestion dan offer dan penjelasannya:

​Lengkapi kalimat berikut:

You ... more fruits and vegetables.... you with your homework?

Jawaban yang tepat adalah:

You should eat more fruits and vegetables. (Kamu harusnya makan buah dan sayur lebih banyak). Can I help you with your homework? (Bisakah saya bantu kamu mengerjakan PR?)

Pembahasan

Di dalam contoh soal di atas tentang suggestion and offer, kita mendapat dua kalimat yang harus dilengkapi. Kalimat tersebut mewakili sebuah suggestion atau saran dan mewakili sebuah offer (tawaran). Kalimat pertama yang harus kita lengkapi adalah:

You ... more fruits and vegetables.

Kalimat ini harus kita lengkapi dengan cara melihat kata-kata yang ada selain yang kosong agar kita tahu konteks kalimat tersebut tentang apa. Dalam hal ini kalimat ini adalah sebuah suggestion, maka kita lengkapi dengan "you should eat".

... you with your homework?

Kalimat kedua ini adalah sebuah offer atau tawaran, dalam hal ini menawarkan untuk membantu mengerjakan tugas PR. Maka kita bisa memberikan kata-kata "can I help" yang merupakan kata-kata paling dasar dalam menawarkan bantuan.

16. Contoh soal Expressing intention


Contoh 1 :

Fera: Hy Elvi!
(Hai Elvi!)
Elvi: Hy Fera!
(Hai Fera!)
Fera: Do you already have any plan for this holiday?
(Apa kamu sudah punya rencana untuk liburan kali ini?)
Elvi: Not really. How about you?
(Tidak juga. Bagaimana denganmu?)
Fera: I am planning to spend my holiday at my hometown.
(Aku berencana untuk menghabiskan waktu kiburan di kampung halamanku.)
Elvi: Your hometown? Where is it?
(Kampung halamanmu? Dimana itu?)
Fera: Lampung. I missed my hometown so much. It has been a year since the last time I visited my hometown.
(Lampung. Aku sangat merindukan kampung halamanku. Sudah satu tahun sejak terakhir kali aku mengunjunginya.)

Contoh 2 :

Adi: What are you doing Tio?
(Apa yang sedang kamu lakukan Tio?)
Tio: Nothing. I am just listening to music.
(Tidak ada. Aku hanya mendengarkan musik.)
Adi: Have you heard that Budi’s house was on fire?
(Sudahkah kamu mendengar bahwa rumah Budi kebakaran?)

Tio: Yes, I have.
(Ya, aku sudah dengar.)
Adi: Don’t you want to help him? I thought you two quite close.
(Apakah kamu tidak ingin menolongnya? Aku pikir kalian berdua cukup dekat.)
Tio: I intended to help him, but he said that he is fine and he doesn’t need my help.
(Aku bermaksud membantunya, tapi dia berkata kalau dia baik-baik saja dan tidak butuh bantuanku.)
Adi: He really said that?
(Dia benar-benar mengatakan itu?)
Tio: Yes, he did.
(Ya, dia mengatakannya.)
Adi: Maybe he got emotional and said that to you. Just let him alone for now.
(Mungkin dia menjadi emosional dan mengatakan hal seperti itu padamu. Biarkan saja dia sendiri untuk sekarang.)
Tio: Yes I know.
(Ya aku tahu.)

Contoh 3 :

Lia: Eva, have you heard that “Music Bank” concert will be held in Indonesia?
(Eva, sudahkah kamu mendengar bahwa konser “Music Bank” akan digelar di Indonesia?)
Eva: No, I haven’t heard that. Really?
(Belum, aku bekum dengar. Benarkah?)
Lia: Yes, really. I intend to save some money from now to buy the ticket.
(Ya, benar. Aku bermaksud untuk menabung dari sekarang untuk membeli tiketnya.)
Eva: I also will do it. So, we can watch the concert together.
(Aku juga akan menabunng. Jadi, kita bisa menonton kensernya bersama-sama.)
Lia: Yeah. That’s great!
(Ya. Itu bagus!)

17. Make expressing an offer! ​


Jawaban:

Here are a few phrases you can use.Offers often begin Would you like…?

Would you like some more candies?

Would you like something to help?

Would you like some rest?

Would you like another emails?

In a more formal style, you can say Can I get…? or May I get…? Expressions like Can I offer you…? or May I offer you…? are also possible.

May I bring you some color pencils?

Can I make you fried rice?

May I offer you something to drink?

May I help you with this?

Offers to do things for people often begin Would you like me to…?

Would you like me to type your assignments for you?

Would you like me to make some handcraft for you?

More examples are given below.

Shall I get you something to eat?

You look tired. Would you like a cup of smoothie?

How about a hot dog?

Can I get you some markers?

Can I get your time?

Can I do something for you?

38. contoh Giving a recommendation, responding a recommendation, expressing an offer, responding an offer


kelas : XI
Pelajaran : Bahasa Inggris
Kategori : Expressions
Kata kunci : giving recommendation, responding a recommendation, giving an offer, responding an offer

pembahasan:
Giving recommendation adalah ungkapan yang menyatakan saran. Sedangkan giving an offer adalah ungkapan yang menyarankan sesuatu.

Rumus giving recommendation expressions pada suasana yang tidak mendesak
- subject + should/ought to + verb1
1) I suggest that you ...
2) I recommend that you ...
3) You really should/ought to ...
4) (I’d say that) You’d better ...
5) If I were you, I would ...
6) It would probably a good idea to ...
7) Why don’t you ...?
8) How about ...?
9) Maybe you should ...
10) I think you should/ought to ...

Contoh giving recommendation:
1) (Formal) : Your shoes looks so dirty. You should clean it.
2) (Formal) : I suggest that we do this project together.
3) (Formal) : I think you should go and help your mother.
4) (Informal) : What about going to the restaurant now?.
5) (Informal) : How about taking the bus instead of driving?

Accepting a recommendation:
1) Yes, I’d like/ love to.
2) That sounds like a good idea.
3) Thank you.
4) Why didn’t I think of that?

Refusing a recommendation:
1) I tried that, but…
2) That’s a good idea, but …
3) No, I’d rather not.
4) Sorry, I can't.

Rumus giving a recommendation dalam situasi yang mendesak:
- Subject + had better + verb1
Contoh :
You look so pale. You had better go to the doctor.

Giving an offer expressions:
- Can I ...?
- May I ...?
- Shall I ...?


Contoh giving an offer:
1) Can I help you?
2) May I read this book for him?
3) Shall I carry your bag to your room?
4) Shall I get you a bottle of water?

Accepting an offer:
1) Yes, please!
2) That would be very nice.
3) Thank you.
4) What kind of you.

Refusing an offer:
1) Thank you, but I can't do it myself.
2) No, thank you.

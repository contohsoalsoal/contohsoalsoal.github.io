Jawaban:

1.) Orientation

Orientation or so-called introduction, contains about who, when, where a story is set.

2.) Complication

Complication tells the beginning of the problem that causes the peak of the problem or what is commonly known as the climax. This section usually involves the main character of the story.

3.)Resolution

This section is the end of the story or in the form of a solution to the problem that occurred. Problems can be solved can be for better or even worse which will make the story end happily or vice versa.

Sometimes, there are resolutions which are other problems to solve. It is deliberately created by the author to increase and maintain interest and tension for the readers. Usually, this type of resolution is found in the mysteries and horror genres.

Re-orientation

The part is the closing of a story which is optional. Re-orientation can contain moral lessons, suggestions or teachings from the author.

4 step in narrative text :

1) Orientation

Orientation or so-called introduction, contains about who, when, where a story is set.

2) Complication

Complication tells the beginning of the problem that causes the peak of the problem or what is commonly known as the climax. This section usually involves the main character of the story.

3) Resolution

This section is the end of the story or in the form of a solution to the problem that occurred. Problems can be solved can be for better or even worse which will make the story end happily or vice versa. Sometimes, there are resolutions which are other problems to solve. It is deliberately created by the author to increase and maintain interest and tension for the readers. Usually, this type of resolution is found in the mysteries and horror genres.

4) Re-orientation

The part is the closing of a story which is optional. Re-orientation can contain moral lessons, suggestions or teachings from the author.

READING COMPREHENSION

1) Simple past tense

2) Once upon time, long time ago, last year.

EXPLANATION

Narrative text adalah genre teks yang dikembangkan berdasarkan alur tertentu yang melibatkan emosi pembacanya. Narrative text pada umumnya disajikan dalam cerita-cerita fiksi : mitos, legenda, fabel dan realita yang didaur ulang dengan tujuan untuk menghibur para pembaca.

GENERIC STRUCTURE

▶Orientation  

Paragraf ini mencakup latar belakang cerita [scene], pengenalan tokoh, waktu terjadi perkara dan keterangan tempat yang ingin dipaparkan dalam cerita.  

▶Complication

Paragraf selanjutnya memuat  konflik, permasalahan -permasalahan yang mulai timbul pada tokoh-tokoh utamanya.

▶Resolution

Paragraf ini memuat  penyelesaian akhir dari permasalahan yang muncul, apakah berakhir happy ending atau sad ending.

▶Coda/Re-Orientation

Berisi tentang pesan-pesan moral positive yang dapat disimpulkan dari kisah/cerita yang disajikan.

LANGUAGE FEATURES

~ Penggunaan keterangan waktu yang spesifik. Pada umumnya dicantumkan di awal pembukaan paragraf utama.

Misalnya : once upon a time..., long time ago, last year.

~Penggunaan struktur kalimat simple past tense (kata kerja bentukan lampau)

~Memuat percakapan - percakapan dengan menggunakan kalimat langsung (direct speech) bentuk present tense.

~ Penggunaan konjungsi temporal.

Misalnya : when, suddenly, then

~ Penggunaan nama² tokoh fiktif yg tidak bersifat umum

3. Tulislah 3 kalimat yang menyatakan expressing sympathy dalam bahasa inggris...​


Jawaban:

1. I heard that your father passed away yesterday. I am sorry to hear that.

2. Your mother told me that you failed in the selection test. I know what you feel, just try again next time.

3. I sympathize about the situation that happened. I hope her condition will get well soon

Penjelasan:

Expressing sympathy (menunjukkan/menyampaikan simpati)

merupakan ungkapan yang digunakan dengan tujuan menyampaikan simpati pada orang yang terkena musibah, berduka cita, dan lain sebagainya.

1. I heard that your father passed away yesterday. I am sorry to hear that.

artinya

(Saya dengar ayah mu meninggal kemarin. Saya turut sedih mendengarnya).

2. Your mother told me that you failed in the selection test. I know what you feel, just try again next time.

artinya

(Ibumu bilang kalau kamu gagal dalam tes seleksi. Aku mengerti perasaanmu, mari coba lagi nanti.)

3. I sympathize about the situation that happened. I hope her condition will get well soon.

artinya

(Saya bersimpati atas situasi yang terjadi. Saya berharap semoga kondisinya segera membaik).

16. buatlah contoh percakapan tentang expressing sympathy and expressing happiness


expressing happiness :
Marie: Mom ! I have a good news!
Mom : What is't, honey ?
Marie : My math teacher told me that I got excellent score in the last test!
Mom : Really? Oh I'm proud of you dear!
Marie: Yeah mom, thanks
Mom : Because you get excellent score. i want to give you a present!
Marie : What is it mom ?
Mom : I will give you a ticket concert!
Marie : Really ? wow i really like it mom. thank you so much mom!
Mom : My pleasure dear.

expressing sympathy :
Marie : Hello! Ann, you look dejected. What’s wrong?
Ann : My grandma passed away in Surabaya, Marie.
Marie : It’s terribly. I am sorry to hear that, Ann. How old was she?
Ann : She was eighty seven years old.
Marie : She was old
Ann : the doctor said that she had been having a hard time with many complications.
Marie: When is her funeral?
Ann : Tomorrow. I am going to buy a ticket to go to Surabaya now. Do you want to accompany me?
Marie : I know how you feel. I am here with you. I will accompany you. Don’t worry.

18. contoh dialog tentang expressing sympathy dan expressing condolence


Lisa : Hi, Jennie. Why didn't you come to school yesterday?
Jennie : Hi, Lisa. My grandfather died yesterday.
Lisa : Oh! I am sorry to hear that. (contoh expressing condolences)
Jennie : Thanks, Lisa. I am still sad.
Lisa : This must be so hard for you. (contoh expresaing sympathy)
Jennie : Sure. He is the best grandpa for me.
Lisa : My deepest condolences upon your loss of your lovely grandpa. (contoh expressing condolence)
Jennie : Thank you so much

A. PAST TENSE VS PAST CONTINUOUS
1. My mother called me last night, when I was studying
2. The children were watching television, when their parents came home
3. You were making dinner, when my friends went home
4. When she was cleaning the windows, her husband called me
5. I was sleeping, when my brother woke me up

Gunakan PAST CONTINUOUS pada kalimat atau kejadian yang terjadi lebih dulu. Dalam bahasa Indonesia bisa diartikan "..... SEDANG MELAKUKAN".

2. He had a luxury car, before he was 30 years old

17. buatlah 5 contoh kalimat Past continous tense,Future continous tense, and present continous tense​


Jawaban:

1. Contoh kalimat past continuous tense digunakan ketika kita membicarakan hal yang sedang terjadi di masa lampau. Bagaimana mengungkapkan kalimatnya dalam bahasa Inggris? 

Kalimat past continuous tense ditandai dengan was/were + V1+ing dan waktu yang lebih spesifik. Kalimatnya juga bisa digunakan bersama dengan simple past atau past continuous juga dengan menggunakan when atau while.

Berikut contoh kalimat bahasa Inggris past continuous tense:

(+) Fina was talking to me when her mother called. / Fina sedang berbicara denganku ketika ibunya menelepon.

(-) Fina was not talking to me when her mother called. / Fina tidak sedang berbicara denganku ketika ibunya menelepon.

(?) Was Fina talking to you when her mother called? / Apakah Fina sedang berbicara denganmu ketika ibunya menelepon?

(+) I was preparing for the exam in October. / Aku sedang mempersiapkan ujian pada bulan Oktober.

(-) I wasn’t preparing for the exam in October. / Aku tidak sedang mempersiapkan ujian pada bulan Oktober.

(?) Were you preparing for the exam in October? / Apakah kamu sedang mempersiapkan ujian pada bulan Oktober?

2. Kalimat past future continuous tense digunakan ketika berbicara mengenai sesuatu yang akan sedang berlangsung di masa lampau.

Kalimat past future continuous tense ditandai dengan would + be + Ving.

Berikut contoh kalimat bahasa Inggris past future continuous tense:

- They would be preparing the presentation if the light didn’t turn off. / Mereka akan mempersiapkan presentasinya jika lampunya tidak mati.

- The teacher said that they should be studying math. / Gurunya berkata bahwa mereka seharusnya belajar matematika.

- The manager said that they would be filming a new drama. / Manajernya berkata bahwa mereka akan syuting drama baru.

3. Apa itu present continuous tense? Melansir British Council, we use the present continuous (am/is/are + -ing) to talk about temporary things which have begun but haven't finished.

Kita menggunakan present continuous (am/is/are + -ing) untuk berbicara tentang hal-hal sementara yang telah dimulai tapi belum selesai.

Contoh kalimat present continuous tense

Berikut contoh kalimat bahasa Inggris present continuous tense:

(+) I am making a glass of ice tea. / Aku sedang membuat segelas es teh.

(-) I am not making a glass of ice tea. / Aku tidak sedang membuat segelas es teh.

(?) Are you making a glass of ice tea? / Apakah kamu sedang membuat segelas es teh?

(+) My brother is studying in his room. / Kakakku sedang belajar di kamarnya.

(-) My brother is not studying in his room. / Kakakku tidak sedang belajar di kamarnya.

(?) Is your brother studying in his room? / Apakah kakakmu sedang belajar di kamarnya?

19. Contoh cerita menggunakan simple past tense past continous tense dan past perfect tense


Pembahasan:


Sebelum membuat cerita yang menggunakan simple past tense, past continuous tense, dan past perfect tense, kita sebaiknya mengetahui struktur kalimat yang menggunakan simple past tense, past continuous tense, dan past perfect tense terlebih dahulu. Berikut penjelasannya.



Simple past tense

Tenses ini digunakan untuk mengekspresikan suatu kejadian atau kegiatan yang dilakukan pada masa lampau.

Struktur kalimat:

(+) S + V2 + O

(-) S + didn't / did not + V1 + O

(?) Did + S + V1 + O + ?

Contoh kalimat: I watched the World Cup on TV last night.



Past continuous tense

Tenses ini digunakan menyatakan suatu kejadian atau kegiatan yang sedang berlangsung di masa lampau.

Struktur kalimat:

(+) S + was / were + V-ing + O

(-) S + wasn't / weren't + V-ing + O

(?) Was / were + S + V-ing + O + ?

Contoh kalimat: I was cooking dinner when you called me yesterday


Past perfect tense

Tenses ini digunakan untuk menyatakan suatu kejadian yang telah terjadi sebelum kejadian lain pada masa lampau.

Struktur kalimat:

(+) S + had + V3 + O

(-) S + had + not + V3 + O

(?) Had + S + V3 + O +?

Contoh kalimat: She had had breakfast before she went to school.


Berikut contoh cerita yang menggunakan simple past tense, past continuous tense, dan past perfect tense.


Last week was my birthday. My family members seemed like they forgot my birthday. They didn't say happy birthday or gave a birthday present for me. They just did their daily routine as usual like there was nothing special. At school, my friends also didn't remember my birthday. It made me so sad. I went home early after the class had finished. I went to my bedroom when i had arrived at home. I cried because i thought nobody remembered my birthday. Suddenly, my bedroom's door opened when i was crying. I saw my family and my friends brought a birthday cake for me. I wiped my tears when they were singing happy birthday. Apparently, they had secretly planned to trick me because they knew that my birthday was coming soon. it was an unforgettable birthday surprise for me.

29. Jelaskan perbedaan present perfect continous tense dengan past continous tense


Present perfect continuous tense
= Digunakan untuk menjelaskan suatu kejadian yang telah terjadi di masa sekarang dan masih berlanjut sampai saat ini.

Formulanya ( pattern ) :
(+) S + has/have + been + v-ing + O + Ts ( if necessary ).
(-) S + has/have + not + been + v-ing + O + Ts ( if necessary ).
(?) Has/have + S + been + v-ing + O + Ts ( if necessary )?

Past continuous tense
= Digunakan untuk menjelaskan kejadian berlanjut yang terjadi di masa lampau.

Formulanya ( pattern ) :
(+) S + was/were + v-ing + O + Ts ( if necessary ).
(-) S + was/were + not + v-ing + O + Ts ( if necessary ).
(?) Was/were + s + v-ing + O + Ts ( if necessary )?

Nb :
> S = subject
> O = object
> Ts = time signal

30. Apa itu past perfect tense dan past perfect continous


Pengertian Past Perfect Tense

Past perfect tense adalah suatu tense yang menggambarkan suatu kejadian yang terjadi sebelum satu spesifik waktu di masa lampau dan telah selesai sebelum kejadian lain yang terjadi di masa lampau juga. Tense ini terdiri dari gabungan aspek perfect (sebelum satu spesifik waktu di masa lampau) dan bingkai waktu past (masa lampau).

Rumus Past Perfect Tense

Tense ini dibentuk dengan bentuk masa lampau dari auxiliary verb have atau has, yaitu had dan past participle (verb 3). Semua subjek baik singular atau plural menggunakan had.

Bentuk Rumus Past Perfect Tense Contoh Kalimat

Positif

(+) · Subjek +  had + verb 3 · Contoh Kalimat: They had studied

Negatif

(-) · Subjek +  had + not + verb 3 · Contoh Kalimat: They had not studied

Interogatif

(?) · Had+ subjek + verb 3? · Contoh Kalimat: Had they studied?


Past Perfect Continous:

Past Perfect Continuous Tense dibentuk dengan auxiliary verb had dan been dan present participle. Berikut rumus Past Perfect Continuous Tense yang dapat dijadikan sebagai panduan Anda dalam membuat suatu kalimat.

• Kalimat positif : Subjek + had + been + V1-ing/ present participle

• Kalimat negatif : Subjek + had + not + been + V1-ing/ present participle

• Kalimat pertanyaan : Had + subjek + been + V1-ing/ present participle + ?

past perfect continous,adalah tenses yang digunakan untuk menyatakan suatu perbuatan itu sedang dilakukan pada masa lampau dan mungkin tidak terjadi lagi.

Present perfect continues tense :

It is used to express an action which started before now (or in the past) and has continued up until now (or into the present)

Past perfect continues tense :

Is used to express a past action which started in the past and continued to happen after another action or time in the past.
